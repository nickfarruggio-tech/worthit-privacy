Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Worth It? mobile application, developed by Nick Farruggio.

Data Collection

Worth It? collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Item names and prices you enter
  • Purchase decisions (bought or passed)
  • Cooling-off period preferences
  • Total savings calculation

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not collect, share, sell, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Analytics and Tracking

Worth It? does not use analytics services, crash reporting, advertising networks, or any third-party SDKs that collect data. We have no way to identify you or observe how you use the app.

Children's Privacy

Worth It? is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children, and the app does not contain content specifically designed for them.

Security

Because your data never leaves your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security — including its lock screen, biometric authentication, and operating system disk encryption. We have no servers that could be breached.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual items at any time within the app. Uninstalling Worth It? will permanently remove all stored data from your device.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will change the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through an in-app notice when possible.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at worthitapp.support@gmail.com.
